Design Principles Document

PARC: Contrast

GitKraken

GitKraken's use of contrast is one of the best in my opinion. I love seeing sites with dark UI but not all of them are pulled of in a pleasing way. GitKraken is able to show visually what is most important and draw the user's attention, while keeping the bright whites to a minimum. They also find ways to fit in lots of colors in a subtle way.

PARC: Proximity

BYU Idaho

Although it has it's quirks, I feel like the BYU-I site does a good job of following the Proximity design principle. The elements that are related are grouped together well and there is ample space between unrelated objects.

PARC: Alignment

GitHub

Github does a great job of aligning elements on their site. There is a lot of reading to be done and having things neatly laid out in a clean pattern while also keeping the text on the left side of the page really improves the experience.
