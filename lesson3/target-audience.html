Site Audience and Scenarios

Target Audience

The target audience of the BCC are residents and visitors around the Dune Sea area including Bestine, up to Mos Espa and down to Anchorhead. Target visitors age ranges between 18 and 100. Visitors' technology includes holocasters and datapads. Income levels will generally be around 50,000 credits annually.

Personas

Sew Oggenn

  • Occupation: Repair woman. Fixes speeders, droids, and occasionally starships
  • Education and Demographics: Has Bachelors degree in mechanical engineering and is 32 years old
  • Goals for site: find activities to do over the weekend
  • Technology: Accesses site mostly through datapad (tablet)

Brul Kaore

  • Occupation: Moisture farmer
  • Education and Demographics: GED, 43 Years old, Widower
  • Goals for site: Find deals on droid repairs and stay up to date on the pod races in Mos Espa
  • Technology: Usually accesses the holonet over a holocaster (desktop) from home
