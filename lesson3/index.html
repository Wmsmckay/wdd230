Site Plan

Site Name

The site will be called Bestine Chamber of Commerce(bestine-chamber-of-commerce.com) or BCC when shortened. It will contain information from businesses and events located within the Dune Sea.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Bestine Chamber of Commerce site is to inform both residents and visitors about ongoing activities and the current state of events. Tatooine is constantly changing and has many active areas and so having a single location to get all of your news will help drive customers to businesses and hopefully, foster more activity in the community.
